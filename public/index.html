Who We Are

EduNova Academy is a smart online platform that helps O/L students learn easily and effectively. We provide subject-wise lessons, interactive tools, and practice materials all in one place, anytime, anywhere.

Our mission is to make quality education accessible to every student, helping them achieve their academic goals through innovative learning methods and comprehensive study resources.

What We Offer

Theory Lessons

Comprehensive topic-wise lessons designed to help students understand complex concepts with clear explanations and examples.

Revision Tutes

Quick revision notes and summaries for last-minute exam preparation, covering all essential topics in an easy-to-digest format.

MCQ Practice

Interactive multiple choice questions to test your knowledge instantly with immediate feedback and detailed explanations.

Final Exam Papers

Previous years' final exam papers for practice, helping students familiarize themselves with exam patterns and question types.

Tutes

Comprehensive tutorial materials and guided practice sessions to reinforce learning and improve understanding of complex topics.

SAP Exams

Simulated Assessment Papers designed to test your knowledge and prepare you for the actual O/L examinations with realistic exam conditions.
